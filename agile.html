What is Agile?

Agile is not:

  • Another Software Methodology
  • Silver bullet for all software problems and environments

Agile ~ Lifestyle

Universal

  • Not from software industry
  • Can be applied to any area of your life

Lean Principles

  • Reducing waste
  • Living optimally
  • Continious Improvement (Kaizen)

Value Driven

  • People are driven by values - not rules or processes
  • Highly sucessfull companies live and breath their vision and mission statements

Where did Agile originate?

  • Not a new concept or idea
  • Not specific to software either
  • Some principles were borrowed from lean
  • Industry experts got together
  • Identified best practices and processes
  • Derived common values they all agreed upon

AGILE MANIFESTO

http://agilemanifesto.org/

Working software over comprehensive documentation

Individuals & interactions over processes & tools

Responding to change over following a plan

Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
